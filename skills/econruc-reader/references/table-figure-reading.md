# Reading Tables and Figures

## Regression tables

Read in this order:

1. Outcome and unit.
2. Key regressor/treatment.
3. Sample and period.
4. Fixed effects and controls.
5. Standard error clustering.
6. Baseline mean and magnitude.
7. Stability across columns.
8. Which identifying threat each column addresses.

Do not summarize only significance stars. Convert coefficients into economic units.

## Event-study figures

Check:

- omitted period
- pre-trends
- treatment timing
- confidence intervals
- dynamic interpretation
- composition under staggered adoption

## Mechanism tables

Ask whether the mechanism is directly identified or only suggestive. Separate mechanism, heterogeneity, and mediation.

## Model or calibration figures

Identify:

- calibrated moments
- targeted vs untargeted moments
- policy counterfactual
- welfare object
- sensitivity range

## Descriptive figures

Ask whether the figure motivates the design, documents a fact, or is being used as causal evidence. Label that role explicitly.
