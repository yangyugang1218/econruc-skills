# Critique and Questions

## Referee-style critique

Organize critique by:

- question and contribution
- identification/model
- measurement and data
- interpretation and mechanism
- external validity
- exposition and positioning

## Good seminar questions

Use questions that reveal a design or interpretation issue:

- What is the relevant counterfactual for this estimate?
- Which units identify the coefficient?
- Could anticipation or selection explain the pre-period pattern?
- How does the effect compare with the baseline mean or policy cost?
- Which competing mechanism would produce the same pattern?
- Is the welfare interpretation local or general?
- What would make the result fail in another setting?

## Improvement suggestions

Prefer actionable suggestions:

- add pre-trend or placebo test
- report baseline means
- clarify sample construction
- convert full table to coefficient plot
- separate mechanism from heterogeneity
- soften causal language
- add literature distinction

Avoid vague comments like `more robustness is needed` without naming the threat.
