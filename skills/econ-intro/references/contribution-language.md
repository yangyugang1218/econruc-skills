# Contribution Language for Economics Introductions

Use contribution language to clarify what the paper teaches economists. Avoid inflated claims such as `fills a gap` unless the gap is explicitly conceptual or empirical.

## English contribution patterns

### Substantive contribution

- `This paper contributes by showing that [economic force] affects [outcome] through [margin].`
- `The results sharpen our understanding of [theory/debate] by isolating [specific channel].`
- `The evidence shows that [policy/institution] changes [behavior] on a margin that is central to [welfare/efficiency/distribution].`
- `The paper documents a previously difficult-to-measure margin of [behavior/adjustment].`
- `The findings suggest that [standard model/policy debate] misses an important role for [constraint/incentive/friction].`

### Identification or data contribution

- `The setting provides plausibly exogenous variation in [treatment/exposure] across [units/time].`
- `The design addresses [endogeneity concern] by comparing [treated units] with [counterfactual group].`
- `The data allow us to observe [margin] at a level of detail not available in prior work.`
- `Unlike previous studies that rely on [aggregate/self-reported/cross-sectional] data, we use [micro/administrative/panel] data to measure [object].`
- `The identifying variation is local to [population/threshold/shock], and we interpret the estimates accordingly.`

### Literature contribution

- `The paper speaks to three literatures.`
- `First, it contributes to work on [literature] by providing evidence on [unresolved margin].`
- `Second, it relates to studies of [related field], for which [mechanism/design] is central.`
- `Third, the findings inform policy debates on [policy] by quantifying [effect/tradeoff/incidence].`
- `Relative to this literature, our contribution is not the setting per se, but the ability to identify [margin/channel].`

## Chinese contribution patterns

### 问题贡献

- `本文的边际贡献首先在于，将……问题推进到……这一更具体的经济行为层面。`
- `本文不是简单考察……是否有效，而是进一步识别其通过何种边际影响……。`
- `这一发现有助于理解……中的激励约束、资源配置或分配效应。`
- `本文为……争论提供了来自……情境的微观经验证据。`

### 数据与识别贡献

- `本文利用……带来的外生/准外生变异，构造……的反事实比较。`
- `相较于已有研究主要依赖……数据，本文使用……数据刻画……。`
- `本文的识别策略有助于缓解……导致的内生性问题。`
- `需要说明的是，本文估计结果主要反映……样本/时期/政策边界下的局部效应。`

### 文献贡献

- `本文与三支文献相关。`
- `第一，本文回应了关于……的研究，重点补充了……方面的证据。`
- `第二，本文拓展了……文献，说明……在……情境下具有重要作用。`
- `第三，本文对……政策讨论具有启示意义，因为它量化了……。`
- `与已有研究相比，本文的贡献不在于更换研究对象，而在于利用……识别……。`

## Language calibration

| Weak or vague | Better |
|---|---|
| `This topic is very important.` | `This margin matters because it affects [allocation/welfare/distribution].` |
| `There is little research on China.` | `Existing evidence is limited because [data/design/measurement] has made [margin] difficult to observe.` |
| `We fill the gap.` | `We provide evidence on [unresolved mechanism/question] using [design/data].` |
| `The policy has a significant effect.` | `The estimates imply a [magnitude] change relative to [baseline].` |
| `本文具有重要意义。` | `本文有助于理解……如何通过……影响……。` |
| `本文丰富了相关研究。` | `本文在……文献中补充了关于……的因果/机制/测量证据。` |

## Verbs by identification strength

- Strong causal design: `estimate the effect of`, `identify`, `show that`, `provide causal evidence`
- Credible but bounded quasi-experiment: `estimate`, `provide evidence that`, `are consistent with a causal interpretation under [assumption]`
- Descriptive or correlational: `document`, `show that X is associated with Y`, `provide descriptive evidence`
- Mechanism without direct identification: `suggest`, `are consistent with`, `point to`, `support the interpretation that`
- Chinese equivalents:
  - `estimate` -> `估计`
  - `document` -> `刻画/呈现`
  - `identify` -> `识别`
  - `are consistent with` -> `与……解释一致`
  - `suggest` -> `表明/提示`, but avoid overstating
