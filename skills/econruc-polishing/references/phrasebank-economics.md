# Economics Phrasebank

## Research question

- `We study whether...`
- `We ask how...`
- `This paper examines the effect of...`
- `The central empirical challenge is...`
- `The key question is not whether [X] matters, but through which margin it affects [Y].`
- `We focus on [margin] because it is central to [welfare/efficiency/distribution].`
- `The paper addresses a simple but unresolved question: ...`
- `Our setting allows us to observe [margin] that is usually difficult to measure.`
- `The analysis distinguishes between [mechanism A] and [mechanism B].`
- `We use [policy/institutional change] to study [economic behavior].`

## Identification

- `We exploit variation in...`
- `The design compares...`
- `Identification comes from...`
- `The identifying assumption is that...`
- `This assumption would be violated if...`
- `To assess this concern, we...`
- `The relevant counterfactual is...`
- `The identifying variation is at the [unit-time] level.`
- `The estimate is local to [compliers/threshold/treated units].`
- `A key threat is differential exposure to...`
- `We probe this threat using...`
- `The design does not require that..., but it does require that...`

## Results

- `The estimates imply...`
- `Relative to the pre-treatment mean, this effect corresponds to...`
- `The coefficient is stable across...`
- `The effect is concentrated among...`
- `We find little evidence that...`
- `The magnitude is economically meaningful: ...`
- `The point estimate changes little after adding...`
- `The confidence interval rules out effects larger than...`
- `The pattern emerges only after...`
- `The result is not driven by...`
- `The estimates are precise enough to distinguish...`

## Mechanisms

- `We next examine whether...`
- `A natural channel is...`
- `This pattern is consistent with...`
- `The evidence points to...`
- `These tests are suggestive rather than definitive because...`
- `The channel predicts that...`
- `We test this implication by examining...`
- `The heterogeneity pattern is consistent with...`
- `This evidence supports the channel, but it does not rule out...`
- `The mechanism appears to operate through [margin] rather than [alternative margin].`

## Robustness and limitations

- `The results are robust to...`
- `A remaining concern is...`
- `This exercise addresses... but does not rule out...`
- `The interpretation is local to...`
- `The evidence should be read as... rather than...`
- `The result is robust to redefining...`
- `The estimate is similar when excluding...`
- `The placebo exercise reduces concern that...`
- `The interpretation is limited by...`
- `The design cannot distinguish between...`
- `The analysis abstracts from...`

## Contribution

- `The paper contributes to...`
- `Relative to prior work, our setting allows us to...`
- `The results speak to...`
- `This evidence complements...`
- `The paper adds to the literature by...`
- `The contribution is to identify [margin], not merely to study [setting].`
- `The setting provides a test of theories in which...`
- `The data allow us to measure... at [frequency/unit].`
- `The paper complements work on... by...`
- `The findings refine the view that...`

## Data and measurement

- `We link [data source] to [data source] at the [unit] level.`
- `The main outcome measures...`
- `The treatment variable captures...`
- `The sample covers [units] from [period].`
- `We aggregate to the [unit-time] level to match the identifying variation.`
- `Measurement error would bias the estimate toward/away from... if...`
- `The baseline sample excludes... because...`
- `The variable is normalized by...`

## Institutional context

- `The institutional feature central to the design is...`
- `The policy changed [incentive/constraint] for [treated units].`
- `The reform was implemented at [level] and affected [population].`
- `Compliance was enforced through...`
- `The setting is useful because...`
- `Two features make this setting empirically attractive. First,... Second,...`
- `The institutional details matter for interpretation because...`

## 中文对照

- `We study whether...` -> `本文考察...是否...`
- `Identification comes from...` -> `识别来自...`
- `The estimates imply...` -> `估计结果意味着...`
- `Relative to the baseline mean...` -> `相对于基准均值...`
- `The evidence is consistent with...` -> `证据与...机制相一致`
- `A remaining concern is...` -> `一个仍需注意的识别威胁是...`
- `The interpretation is local to...` -> `这一解释主要适用于...`
- `The paper contributes to...` -> `本文对...文献的贡献在于...`
