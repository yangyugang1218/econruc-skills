# Evidence to Claim

## Claim strength ladder

### Verified fact

Use when the data source directly supports the statement.

Examples:

- `According to [source], X reached Y in 2023.`
- `数据显示，2023年X为Y。`

### Interpretation

Use when the claim follows from data but requires reasoning.

Examples:

- `This suggests that...`
- `这表明...可能正在成为主要约束。`

### Estimate

Use when the number is calculated from assumptions or multiple sources.

Must include:

- formula
- input sources
- assumptions
- uncertainty

### Forecast

Use only with scenario assumptions.

Never present forecasts as facts.

## Overstatement repair

Weak:

`该政策显著促进了产业升级。`

Safer:

`现有数据表明，该政策实施后相关投资和产出指标有所改善；但若要识别其因果影响，还需要进一步控制同期需求和地方产业基础差异。`

Weak:

`The market will grow rapidly.`

Safer:

`Under the baseline assumptions on demand growth and policy continuity, the market is likely to continue expanding, although the pace depends on financing conditions and implementation capacity.`

## Evidence map

Before drafting a section, map:

```text
Claim -> Evidence -> Source -> Inference -> Caveat -> Report sentence
```

If the evidence or source is missing, do not draft the claim as a fact.
