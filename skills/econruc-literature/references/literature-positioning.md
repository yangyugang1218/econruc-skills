# Literature Positioning

## Literature map

For each literature, capture:

- question
- core finding
- identification or model
- unresolved margin
- how the user's paper contributes

Template:

| Literature | What it has shown | Remaining margin | This paper's contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| [topic] | [established evidence] | [gap as question] | [design/data/mechanism contribution] |

## Related-work paragraph

Template:

`A first literature studies [question]. Existing work shows [finding], often using [setting/design]. Less is known about [margin]. This paper contributes by [contribution].`

## Contribution statement

Strong contribution statements are specific:

- `by identifying the effect of [policy] on [margin]`
- `by measuring [object] at [unit/frequency]`
- `by distinguishing [mechanism A] from [mechanism B]`
- `by testing whether [known mechanism] travels to [setting]`

Weak statements are topic-based:

- `by studying China`
- `by enriching the literature`
- `by filling a gap`

## Chinese synthesis

Use:

`第一类文献关注...，已有研究主要发现...。然而，关于...仍缺乏直接证据。本文利用...，从...角度补充了这一文献。`

Avoid:

`国内外学者从不同角度进行了丰富研究。`
